The Best Movies According to Sabina

My top 3 of all-time

The Host, 2013

American romantic science fiction thriller film
based on the 2008 novel of the same name by Stephenie Meyer.

The Help, 2011

A period drama film written and directed by Tate Taylor
and based on Kathryn Stockett's 2009 novel of the same name.

Love, Rosie, 2014

A romantic comedy-drama film directed by Christian Ditter from a screenplay by Juliette Towhidi,
based on the 2004 novel Where Rainbows End by Irish author Cecelia Ahern.
